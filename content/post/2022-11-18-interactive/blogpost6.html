An idea that we have for our interactive is making an interactive map of California where you can zoom in on counties. We can subset maps where you can select by race/ethnicity, age, number of hospitalizations, etc, which would make it more personal and individual. We could also combine our datasets with tidycensus to get more info to use. So far, the progress we have made is an outline of a map for California including the counties, but it is yet to be interactive. We also still are in the process of incorporating our data onto this map, as we are still figuring out leaflet, shiny, etc.
